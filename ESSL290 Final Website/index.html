Free Speech & Civility Concepts for Programmers

Why this website was built

This website was primarily built because I wanted to dip my toes into the realm of HTML and Cascading Style Sheets. I am enrolled in a Free Speech and Civility Milestone Class, and we have been tasked with creating an artifact that is relevant to our studies. With this in mind, I built this website to bring together everything I've learned this semester and show how it applies to programmers. Additionally, I built this artifact as a potential resumé item which demonstrates my capabilities of working with unfamiliar programming languages.
